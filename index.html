The studio

One maker, one cat, lots of ideas.

TheCrazyCat is a small independent developer in the UK, building Android apps that are playful to use and genuinely helpful. The goal is simple: apps that do one thing brilliantly, look good doing it, and respect your time.

BingoNingo was the first family — a full bingo system for clubs, pubs, charities and front-room games. QuizBlitz and RingReminder are next out of the workshop.
